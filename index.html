ABOUT CAFE GATO

Founded by Maximo and Kaylin Mugica-Drumm, Cafe Gato is a unique space where coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts can come together and enjoy a cozy, welcoming environment. Our vision is to create a warm and inviting cafe for our guests while providing a separate, dedicated space for our feline friends. This allows you to savor your favorite beverages while enjoying the delightful company of cats

OUR MISSION

We believe in the therapeutic power of cats and coffee. Our cafe aims to provide a stress-free environment where people can relax, unwind, and find joy in the company of our furry companions. The benefits of our cat cafe extend beyond our human guests.

BENEFITS

Partnering with our local animal shelter, we offer a larger, cage-free environment for our cats to socialize and build confidence around people and other cats. This environment helps improve their overall well-being and increases their chances of finding their forever home.

Spending time with cats has been shown to reduce stress and anxiety, improve mood, and offer a unique and enjoyable experience. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or a family, our café offers a little slice of happiness and relaxation for all!
